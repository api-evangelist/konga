Some conversations take work. You meet someone for the first time and spend the next few minutes figuring out where they’re from, what they do, what they believe, and whether you have anything in common. Sometimes, despite your best efforts, the conversation never quite finds its rhythm. Sport is different. Sport has a way of cutting through all of that.
